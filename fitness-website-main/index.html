    Muscle Gain Plan

    • 🍚 High Carbs + Protein

    💡 Healthy Eating Tips

    • ✔️ Eat every 3-4 hours to maintain metabolism.
    • ✔️ Stay hydrated with at least 2-3L of water daily.
    • ✔️ Avoid processed and deep-fried foods.
    • ✔️ Sleep well – your body recovers and burns fat while you rest.
    • ✔️ Include fiber-rich foods like fruits and whole grains.
